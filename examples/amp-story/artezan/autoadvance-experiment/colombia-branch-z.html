THINGS TO DO IN COLOMBIA

🇨🇴

TAKE A MUDBATH INSIDE AN ACTIVE VOLCANO!

The El Totumo Volcano can be found 45 minutes outside Cartagena The mud apparently has healing and therapeutic properties!

EAT INSIDE A WOMEN'S PRISON!

At Restaurante Interno, the inmates cook and serve you food. Money from the restaurant goes towards skill training for the inmates and many end up working at top restaurants in Cartagena after they’re released.

EXPLORE CARTAGENA'S
OLD TOWN

Get lost on the picturesque streets of Cartagena and admire the colourful architecture, the famous overhanging balconies and the vibrant street life.

ISLAND HOP AROUND ROSARIO ISLANDS

The perfect escape from the city of Cartagena, snorkel in the crystal blue waters, see one of Pablo Escobar’s sunken airplanes and relax on lush beaches.

 BOOK THIS TRIP!

EXPLORE THE VIBRANT
COMUNA 13

Once one of the most dangerous neighbourhoods in Medellín, Comuna 13 has transformed through the help of its passionate community and is now a creative hub of Colombia, with streets packed full of street art.

TAKE A BOAT
TO SEE
PABLO ESCOBAR'S ABANDONED HOUSE

Visit ‘La Manuela’, Pablo Escobar’s second favourite home that was bombed by the rival gang ‘Los Pepes’.

VISIT THE COLOURFUL TOWN OF GUATAPE

A 2 hour drive from Medellín, Guatape is often called 'the most colourful town in Colombia.'

CLIMB THE ICONIC ‘LA PIEDRA DEL PEÑOL’

A short drive from Guatape is 'The Rock’ which has over 700 steps to reach the top and is 200m tall. A tiring journey but the views are definitely worth it!

VISIT A FAMILY RUN COFFEE FARM

For all the coffee lovers out there, learn all about the coffee making process, from seed to the first sip on a lovely family owned coffee farm. You even get to taste it at the end too!
